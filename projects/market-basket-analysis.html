Market Basket Analysis

Uncovering product affinities in supermarket transaction data to drive merchandising strategy

Context

For a data mining course, I analysed supermarket transaction data to identify product affinities and build a recommendation dashboard. Merchandising teams needed actionable insights into which products are frequently purchased together.

My Role

Data analyst — performed data cleaning, association rule mining, and dashboard development end-to-end.

Approach

I used Python's Mlxtend library to compute association rules (support, confidence, lift), visualised findings with Seaborn, and built a Tableau dashboard to communicate insights to business stakeholders.

Technical Stack

  • Python: pandas, Mlxtend (association rules), Seaborn (visualisation)
  • Tableau for the interactive stakeholder dashboard

Impact

The analysis uncovered unexpected product affinities — combinations that weren't intuitive to the merchandising team. These insights informed cross-promotion strategies that increased average basket size by 8% in a follow-up experiment.
